## 1. Overview

This Refund Policy explains our policy regarding refunds for LedgerAI subscriptions. Please read this policy carefully before subscribing to our Service.

LedgerAI operates on a monthly subscription model. All subscriptions are billed in advance on a monthly basis.

## 2. Subscription Model

### 2.1 Monthly Billing

- All subscriptions are billed monthly in advance
- We do not offer annual plans or prepaid subscriptions
- Each billing cycle is one (1) month from your subscription date
- Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled

### 2.2 No Refunds for Partial Periods

**We do not provide refunds for partial billing periods.** Once a subscription fee has been charged, you will have access to the Service for the entire billing period, even if you cancel during that period.

## 3. Cancellation Policy

### 3.1 How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription at any time through:
- Your account settings in the Service
- Contacting us at contact.ledgerai@gmail.com

### 3.2 Cancellation Effective Date

When you cancel:
- Your cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
- You will continue to have access to the Service until the end of the period for which you have already paid
- No refunds are provided for the remaining time in your current billing period
- Your subscription will not renew after the current period ends

### 3.3 Immediate Cancellation

If you cancel your subscription, you will retain access to all features until the end of your current billing period. We do not provide prorated refunds for early cancellation.

## 4. Refund Eligibility

### 4.1 General Policy

**We do not offer refunds for subscription fees that have already been charged**, except in the limited circumstances described below.

### 4.2 Exceptions

We may, at our sole discretion, provide refunds in the following circumstances:

#### 4.2.1 Duplicate Charges
If you are charged multiple times for the same billing period due to a technical error on our part, we will refund the duplicate charges.

#### 4.2.2 Service Unavailability
If the Service is unavailable for more than 48 consecutive hours due to our error (excluding scheduled maintenance), you may request a prorated refund for the affected period. This does not apply to:
- Scheduled maintenance
- Force majeure events
- Issues caused by third-party services
- User error or account issues

#### 4.2.3 Billing Errors
If you are charged incorrectly due to our error (e.g., wrong plan price, incorrect billing cycle), we will refund the overcharge.

#### 4.2.4 Special Circumstances
We may consider refunds in exceptional circumstances on a case-by-case basis. Contact us to discuss your situation.

### 4.3 Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will **not** be provided for:
- Change of mind or dissatisfaction with the Service
- Failure to use the Service during a billing period
- Cancellation during an active billing period
- Upgrading or downgrading your plan (see Section 5)
- Technical issues resolved within 48 hours
- Issues caused by user error, account security breaches, or violation of Terms of Service
- Third-party service outages (e.g., Stripe, Firebase)

## 5. Plan Changes

### 5.1 Upgrading Your Plan

When you upgrade your plan:
- You will be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your current billing period
- The upgrade takes effect immediately
- No refunds are provided for downgrading after an upgrade

### 5.2 Downgrading Your Plan

When you downgrade your plan:
- The downgrade takes effect at the start of your next billing period
- You will continue to have access to your current plan's features until the end of your current billing period
- No refunds are provided for the difference in pricing

## 6. Chargebacks and Disputes

### 6.1 Chargeback Policy

If you dispute a charge with your bank or credit card company (chargeback):
- We will investigate the chargeback
- If the chargeback is found to be invalid, your account may be suspended or terminated
- We reserve the right to dispute chargebacks that we believe are invalid

### 6.2 Resolving Disputes

We encourage you to contact us directly at contact.ledgerai@gmail.com to resolve any billing issues before initiating a chargeback. We are committed to working with you to resolve disputes fairly.

## 7. Processing Refunds

### 7.1 Refund Processing Time

If a refund is approved:
- Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your bank or credit card company

### 7.2 Refund Method

Refunds are processed through Stripe to the original payment method used for the subscription. We cannot issue refunds to a different payment method or account.

## 8. Free Trials and Promotional Periods

### 8.1 Free Trials

If we offer a free trial:
- You will not be charged during the free trial period
- You may cancel before the trial ends to avoid charges
- If you do not cancel, you will be charged at the end of the trial period
- No refunds are provided for charges after a free trial

### 8.2 Promotional Pricing

If you subscribe during a promotional period:
- The promotional price applies to your subscription
- Standard refund policies apply
- Promotional pricing may not be combined with refunds

## 9. Account Termination and Refunds

### 9.1 Termination by Us

If we terminate your account for violation of our Terms of Service:
- No refunds will be provided
- You will lose access to the Service immediately
- You may still be responsible for charges already processed

### 9.2 Termination by You

If you terminate your account:
- Standard cancellation and refund policies apply
- You may request data export before termination
- No refunds are provided for the current billing period

## 10. Subscription Renewal and Refunds

### 10.1 Automatic Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew each month. If you do not want to renew:
- Cancel your subscription before the renewal date
- No refunds are provided for automatic renewals that you did not intend to authorize

### 10.2 Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
- We may suspend your account
- You will be notified to update your payment method
- No refunds are provided for service interruptions due to failed payments
